Projects & Work Experience

DRAFT5.GG
2017 - present DRAFT5.GG is the leading CS:GO content esports platform in Brazil.

I've created this project in Jan/2017. In almost 3 years of it's existence, we expanded our team with social media designers, an editorial team and our numbers got bigger.

On Jun/2019, we've been acquired by Gamers Club and Immortals Gaming Club. This has allowed me to pursue the development of DRAFT5 full-time.

DRAFT5 is a full fledged application created for the CS:GO fans in Brazil. We provide match and tournament coverage, with streams, scores and schedules.

Besides that, we have a editorial team that covers all tournaments in Brazil and around the world.

DRAFT5 is the biggest specialized CS:GO news vehicle in Brazil.

The application consists of:

  • A React-Redux front-end, with direct integration with the API
  • A Node.js API, using express and interacting with REDIS and a MySQL database
  • A React-Redux admin panel, with direct integration with the API, to manage matches, tournaments and teams inside the platform.
